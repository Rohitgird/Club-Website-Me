Come be a part of us.

CODE

Computer programming is what lies at the core of IECSE, and hence it conducts a plethora of events and workshops to uplift the coding culture at MIT, trying to bring it at par with the IIT's and other leading institutions.


IECSE organizes Codemeets that are weekly meetings to enhance one's coding skills which are of great help in competetive coding. The meetings take you through various concepts and algoriths which help you crack even the toughest of problems with high efficiency.

WEB

IECSE arguably houses the best Web Developers on the campus. From amazing websites like the one you are on right now, developed by the WEB Team, from the bottom up, to interactive online events like CyberHawk which is an online Treasure Hunt, IECSE does it all.

IECSE also organizes several workshops and events based on web development, like 'Weeb Week' and 'Web Meets' which help even those even who are new to the field, build professional websites with ease.

DESIGN

AT IECSE, we beleive that "design is not what it looks like. Design is how it works."


IECSE has it's own design studio which consisits of innovative enthusiasts who work together to make MIT a more artistic place. It provides an enviroment for budding designers to learn, grow and to enable you to work with the best designers the college has to offer, and in the process lets you share and fabricate your thoughts.

"Research is creating new knowledge."

Research is an initiative to encourage development in advanced leatning and research. It aims to involve students in learning about various fields of Computer Science like Cryptography, Data Mining, Machine Learning and more. This gives exposure, so one could start working on a project or a research paper in these fields.

HASHTAG

Hashtag is IECSE's very own technical blog. Not only does it contain amazing articles, penned by our own in-house team, but also a technical view on what's happening around manipal.


All new accomplished and projects undertaken by students and faculty of Manipal University are also present.

GOOGLE DEVELOPERS GROUP

GDG OR Google Developer Groups are for those who are intrested in Google's Developer Technology; everything from Android, Chrome, Drive and Google Cloud platforms, to product API's like Cast API, Map API and Youtube API.

GDG can take many forms, from just a few people getting together to watch our latest video to large gatherings with demos and tech talk , to events like code sprints and hackathons.
